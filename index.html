About

From bot scripts to business backbones.

In 2017, I was handed a quietly unusual brief: predict what a person might ask a chatbot, in every phrasing they might use, and write back in a way that didn't feel like talking to a wall. I mapped intent. I trained tone. I learned, early, that good AI conversation is mostly good listening, written down.

That instinct never left — even through a decade in IT, a UI/UX diploma, a nutrition certification, and an ongoing psychology degree. Today I bring all of it back to the same table: using AI tools to help small, independent businesses sound like themselves — clear, warm, and present — at every customer touchpoint.

I work behind the scenes, by design. You don't need another person on another call. You need flows that work, scripts that sound right, and a system that frees up your time — delivered quietly, so you can stay focused on your own clients.
