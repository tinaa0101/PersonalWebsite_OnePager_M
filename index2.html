About

From client floors to bot scripts — one thread runs through it.

My career began in 2007, in IT services — two years in sales, followed by eight and a half years across project management, business analysis, and client management, eventually leading teams. It was a startup, so I ended up doing a bit of everything: requirement gathering, invoicing, new business generation, client delivery. That range took me to Birmingham (UK) and Abu Dhabi for client projects, and gave me something more valuable than any single skill — a sharp instinct for understanding what a client actually needs, often before they've fully said it.

In 2017, that instinct met a new kind of brief: design the conversations a chatbot would have with real users. Predict their intent, map every natural way they might phrase the same question, and train responses that didn't feel robotic. It was conversational AI before the term was common — and it's the thread that's pulled me back to AI today.

In between, I've stayed a committed learner — a UI/UX diploma, a nutrition diploma, an ongoing psychology degree, and a yoga certification (RYT-200, 2023) that I now practice for myself, not as a teacher. Each one feeds the same underlying skill: understanding how people think, feel, and respond.

Today, I bring all of it to one focused offering — helping solo-entrepreneurs use AI tools to communicate better with their own clients, without needing to become AI experts themselves.

What I Do

Three ways I help you sound human.

Built for solo-entrepreneurs and solo-business owners — yoga teachers, nutritionists, caterers, coaches, and creators who don't have time to manage another tool.

01

Conversation & Bot Scripting

I rewrite robotic chatbot replies and FAQ flows into natural, on-brand conversations — anticipating how your customers actually ask, not how a manual assumes they will.

02

AI Workflow Setup

Auto-responders, booking confirmations, lead follow-ups — built once using AI tools, so they keep running quietly in the background while you focus on clients.

03

Prompt & Content Refinement

If you're using AI tools but the output feels flat or off-brand, I refine the prompts and structure so what comes out sounds like you, not a template.
